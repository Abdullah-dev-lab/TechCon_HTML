History

TechCon SoCal launched in the early 2020s, spearheaded by Expert Dojo—a SoCal-based startup accelerator and venture community—and Startup San Diego. It began as a niche gathering focused on bringing together investors, entrepreneurs, and emerging tech innovators across Southern California .

Over the years, TechCon has evolved into a premier tech conference, attracting thousands of attendees from around the globe. The event now features keynote speakers from leading tech companies, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities designed to foster collaboration and innovation.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders and innovators. We aim to provide a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and collaboration that drives technological advancement and entrepreneurship.
